Important Instructions:
 
Dear Candidate,
 

Based on your merit and choices of Institutions & Trades opted by you during online application, you have been provisionally allotted a seat in the above-mentioned Institute and Trade.
Kindly report to Allotted institute within the stipulated period, as mentioned in the schedule, failing which the allotted seat will be cancelled and you will be opted out of all the forthcoming rounds of ITI online admission.
1.

Candidates are required to produce all original documents at the time of reporting at the Allotted Institute as mentioned along with the requisite fees. Documents will be kept in the custody of Institute until the PRN generation on SIDH portal.
2.

Reported Candidates are eligible for upward movement, they have to apply online through E-Mitra/ SSO for upward movement as per the given schedule after 1st allotment.
3.

After allotment of new trade/institute in upward movement Candidate must report to Allotted trade/institute within the stipulated period, as per the given schedule, failing which the allotted seat will be cancelled from previously allotted seat and new alloted seat after upward movement.
